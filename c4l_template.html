For cooking and meal ideas - try the Meal Mixer!

If you're stuck for some healthier meal ideas and need some help getting started, our Meal Mixer can help! Most recipes feed four adults and can be made on a budget.

Use it to choose meal combinations - it will even create a shopping list for you! Or if you just want ideas for a meal today, click on the recipe finder instead - you can browse by meal type or search by ingredient.
